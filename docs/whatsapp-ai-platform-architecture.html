Architecture Brief

WhatsApp AI Platform

Multi-country, multi-vertical workflow platform designed to start with WhatsApp-first booking and lead recovery, then expand into broader SMB operations without rewriting the core.

V1 Focus

Independent beauty professionals and small salons in the UK and UAE.

Core Bet

WhatsApp is the first channel. Workflow automation is the actual product.

Shape

Modular monolith with clear internal boundaries and adapter-based integrations.

Layers

Channels

WhatsApp first, then Instagram DM, web chat, SMS, email, and missed-call recovery.

Orchestration

Canonical event routing, workflow selection, context loading, and state persistence.

Policy

Consent, quiet hours, confidence thresholds, escalation rules, and country-specific behavior.

AI

Intent detection, extraction, drafting, summarization, and tone adaptation only.

Workflow Engine

Booking, reminders, waitlist fill, review requests, and later dispatch/support packs.

Integrations

Booking tools, calendars, CRM, payments, reviews, and notifications behind adapter interfaces.

Phase Plan

Phase 1

Independent beauty professionals. WhatsApp-only. Booking, confirmation, reminder, reschedule.

Phase 2

Small salons. Staff and service routing, waitlist fill, no-show recovery, deposits.

Phase 3

Clinics and med spas. Stronger approvals, multilingual flows, richer analytics.

Phase 4

Home services. Quote capture, dispatch workflows, image-based issue triage.

Design Rules

  • Keep provider-specific behavior inside adapters.
  • Do not let AI write authoritative workflow state directly.
  • Make country behavior declarative, not hard-coded.
  • Keep the product centered on workflows, not on a generic chatbot.
